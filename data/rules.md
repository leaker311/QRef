# Weather

## Alterate Required

- 1hr, 2000ft, 3sm
- Flag: anytime flight is longer than 6hrs regardless of wx
- Flag: ceiling is less than 1500ft above lowest min. or 2000ft
- Flag: vis is less than 2+approach vis, or 3sm
- VCTS, or TS
- steady state wind more than 35kts
- gusts more than 20kts
- Anytime runway conditions may cause ground stop

## Exemption 15466 (no alt required)

- 112 -> 1hr, ceiling 1000ft, vis 2sm (NO TS, must be CAT II, III capable)
- 113 -> 1hr, ceiling 1000ft, vis 3sm (NO TS, must have CAT I)

# Landing

## LAHSO Limitations

- Runway must be dry
- Must have some sort of vertical guidance
- At night MUST have Papi/Vasi
- Wx better than 1500/5 (1000/3 if Papi/Vasi avail)
- No windshear
- 3kt tailwind max

## Maximum Allowable Clutter

- Water or Slush -> 1 Inch
- Wet Snow -> 2 Inches
- Dry Snow -> 6 Inches

# Emergencies

## Land at Nearest Suitable

- Engine Failure
- Engine/APU Fire
- Wheel Well Fire
- Cabin Smoke/Fire that does not go away
- One AC System remaining
- One Hydr System remaining (STBY Sys counts)

# Enroute

## Route Changes Contact Dispatch when:

- Lateral deviations greater than 100NM
- Vertical deviations greater than 4000ft
- ETA later than 15mins

# New FO

## Less than 175hr can't land

- Special Airport (SIP)
- Less than 3/4 Vis
- RVR less than 4000
- Standing water, slush, snow
- Braking less than GOOD
- XWind more than 15kts
- Windshear reported

# Wake Turbulence

## Distances

- 7NM Cat A, A380
- 5NM Cat B, B747/777, A330
- 3.5NM Cat C, MD11, B767
- 3NM Cat D, B757
- 10-30NM behind and 1000ft below

# Fuel

## Burn Rules of Thumb

- APU -> 4lbs/min (250/hr)
- APU and One Eng -> 17lbs/min (1000/hr)
- Two Eng Idle -> 25lbs/min (1500/hr)
- GA and Pattern -> 2000lbs
- Holding NG -> 75lbs/min (4500/hr)
- Holding MAX -> 63lbs/min (3800/hr)

# Terminating

## Times

- 30 secs between IRS off and AC off
- NG 2 minutes between APU off and Bat off
- MAX 5 minutes between APU off and Bat off
