# Weather

## Low Visibility

- If visibility is less than 200m
- Turn on high intensity runway lights
- Notify tower immediately

## High Winds

- If crosswind > 25 knots
- Halt all crane operations
- Secure loose cargo

# Fuel

## Reserve Check

- If fuel < 30 mins remaining
- Declare minimum fuel state
- divert to nearest alternate

# Emergency

## Engine Fire

- Cut Fuel immediately
- Discharge Extinguisher
- Notify ATC "Mayday"

# Crew

## Flight Time

- Max 8 hours per day
- Min 12 hours rest

# Cargo

## Hazmat

- Check lithium battery logs
- Verify secure tie-downs
